Join Us

Come Let's Work Together To Make Our Cyber Space Secure.

Become a part of the Cyber Security Club at the University of Toledo and embark on a journey of learning, growth, and networking. Our club offers a variety of opportunities for students to develop their cybersecurity skills, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to the field.

**Benefits of joining:**

  • Gain practical experience and knowledge
  • Network with industry professionals
  • Participate in exciting events and activities
  • Develop leadership skills
  • Build a strong professional network

**How to join:**

  • Join and follow Club for Cyber Security in InvoNet
  • Attend one of our meetings
  • Have a inperson communication with one of our Team Members
  • Attend meetings and contribute as you can

**Follow us on social media:**

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn

We encourage you to join our club and become a part of the cybersecurity community at the University of Toledo.
